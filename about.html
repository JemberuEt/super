About Us

Super Double 'T' General Trading PLC was established back in 1999 according to the Ethiopian calendar. The company operates as a holding entity, overseeing a diverse array of business activities and entities under its umbrella. From manufacturing and importing to contracting, wholesale, and retail, Super Double 'T' has its hands in a wide range of commercial ventures.

The company's core business sectors include Super Fiber Glass Products, the Rodas Paint Industry, Super water proofing works, the Super Rubber Seal Factory, and Pomy Marble Factory. This diversification across various market segments is underpinned by the organization's extensive market research and rich business experience.

At the heart of Super Double 'T' General Trading PLC's success lies its most valuable asset - its human resources. The management team brings a wealth of inspiration, unwavering commitment, and deep expertise gained through work in private enterprises, government agencies, public companies, non-profits, and international organizations. By leveraging this collective knowledge and experience, the company continually seeks to grow and enhance its performance over time.

The introduction highlights Super Double 'T' General Trading PLC's focus on harnessing the capabilities of its seasoned leadership to drive the organization's ongoing development and expansion across its diverse business interests. The company's strategy centers on translating its market insights and operational know-how into the creation and distribution of high-quality, in-demand products and services.
